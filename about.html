Behind the Lens

I'm Sinalo Gamedze aka SG Trey co-founder at Ledoit Moments, a visual storyteller from Mpumalanga, Nhlazatshe, South Africa. Through the lens of my camera, I capture not just images — but emotion, energy, and essence. My work blends documentary realism with cinematic flair.

What I Do

  • High-end Photography (Portraits, Brands, Events)
  • Cinematic Videography (Weddings, Documentaries, Ads)
  • Video Editing
  • Location Scouting & Creative Direction

My Creative Mission

I help individuals, couples, and brands in Southern Africa and beyond tell their stories in powerful visuals. My craft is rooted in patience, precision, and passion — every shoot is a collaboration, every frame a legacy.

Based in Mphumalanga, Shooting Nationwide

From intimate weddings in Mphumalanga to creative shoots in Gauteng — I’m mobile, equipped, and ready to capture your story anywhere in Southern Africa.

My Journey

  • 2020 — I was blessed with my first camera and I started my photography & Videography journey..It was exciting because it was a dream come true. For the rest of the year I was doing promotional photoshoots and trying to get my name and contacts to as many people as possible.
  • 2021 — This is when I started having my camera make some income for me..I started getting bookings for birthdays,babyshowers and weddings..It was every nice experience.Having to get paid for doing what you love the most.
  • 2023 — I continued to spread the name and I was getting more bookings than the previous year..I started to buy more equipment for my work to improve the quality..
  • 2025 — We've grown so much and we have made Ledoit Moments a trusted brand..The aim is to give our customers the best quality and creative visuals..And we are excited with the future and the improvements that we always work towards
