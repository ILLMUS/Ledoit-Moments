Terms of Use

Last updated: October 2025

1. Bookings & Payment

  • All bookings require a non-refundable deposit to secure your date.
  • Final payment is due on or before the shoot day unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • We accept EFT, cash, or mobile money payments (MoMo/eWallet).

2. Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancellations must be made at least 72 hours before your session to reschedule.
  • Deposits are transferable once, within 30 days of the original booking.
  • Late rescheduling (under 48 hours) may result in additional charges.

3. Delivery & Turnaround Time

  • Standard delivery of edited photos is within 5–10 business days.
  • Video projects may require 10–21 business days depending on complexity.
  • Express delivery is available at an additional fee.

4. Creative Direction & Edits

  • Clients are encouraged to share a mood board or references before the shoot.
  • All editing is done in our signature cinematic style unless otherwise agreed.
  • Only light retouching is included; additional edits may be quoted separately.

5. Use of Content

  • Ledoit Moments reserves the right to use selected visuals for portfolio, marketing, and promotional purposes (including social media and website).
  • If you require private or NDA-bound delivery, please communicate in advance — additional charges may apply.

6. Liability Disclaimer

While every effort is made to deliver exceptional service, Ledoit Moments is not liable for:

  • Unforeseen delays due to weather, power outages, or equipment failure
  • Lost or corrupted files caused by external interference
  • Uncooperative environments or individuals during a shoot
